Brand: McLaren
Year: 2018
About: The Senna is largely based on the McLaren 720S, using a modified version of its carbon fiber monocoque and engine. The Senna is powered by a modified version of the McLaren 720S' twin-turbocharged V8 engine. The Senna does not use an electric motor in favor of its low claimed dry weight of 2,641 lb, which allows for 658 hp.
